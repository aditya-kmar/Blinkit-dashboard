# Blinkit-dashboard
🛒 Blinkit Sales Analysis Dashboard | Power BI

📊 An interactive Power BI dashboard designed to analyze Blinkit's sales performance, customer preferences, outlet distribution, and product trends. This project transforms raw retail data into meaningful business insights, helping stakeholders make data-driven decisions.

🚀 Project Highlights

✅ Analyzed $1.20M+ Total Sales across multiple outlet types and locations.

✅ Evaluated 8,523 products with an average sales value of 141 and customer rating of 3.9.

✅ Identified sales contribution by:

Outlet Size (Small, Medium, High)
Outlet Location (Tier 1, Tier 2, Tier 3)
Outlet Type
Item Type
Fat Content Categories

✅ Tracked outlet establishment trends over multiple years to understand business growth patterns.

✅ Created dynamic filters for deeper exploration and user-friendly analysis.

📈 Key Insights

🔹 Supermarket Type 1 generated the highest revenue, contributing nearly $0.79M in total sales.

🔹 Tier 3 outlets recorded the highest sales performance compared to Tier 1 and Tier 2 locations.

🔹 High-sized outlets contributed the largest share of total revenue.

🔹 The business achieved its peak sales performance around 2018, reaching approximately $205K.

🔹 Regular fat products generated higher sales compared to low-fat products.

🔹 Fruits, Snacks, Household, and Frozen Food categories emerged as the top-performing product segments.
